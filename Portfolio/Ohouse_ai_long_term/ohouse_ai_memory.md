# OHOUSE AI MEMORY

## Overview
Knowledge consolidation from months of Ohouse AI strategy discussions. Each theme links to source conversation files (ohouse_ai_convo_history/XX.md) for reference during pitch development.

---

## 1. Visualization Gap & User Problem
**Summary:** Users find abundant inspiration but lack confidence executing in their own space, creating a decision/execution gap that prevents purchase action.

**Source files:** 02, 03, 04, 10, 16, 18

---

## 2. Visualization & Digital Twin Technology
**Summary:** Room Planner + AR scanning + LLM-based AI generates accurate 3D simulations and furniture recommendations beyond manual effort, with acceptable rendering time (1 min per trial).

**Source files:** 04, 05, 06, 16

---

## 3. Market Research & Competitive Data
**Summary:** 3D visualization in real estate/staging proves market demand: 20-31% faster sales, 87% view increase, 73% faster transaction closure, 6-10% price premium, up to 3600% ROI.

**Source files:** 03, 04, 05, 06

---

## 4. Product Vision & Features
**Summary:** Core vision "Turn inspiration into confident action" via AI styling, Room Planner 3D integration, AR scanning, budget planning, contractor cost estimates, and commerce integration for execution.

**Source files:** 10, 16, 18

---

## 5. Business Model & Monetization
**Summary:** Primary: B2B subscription ($99-$1,500/month) to real estate platforms. Secondary: Consumer free tier drives traffic, ads/affiliate revenue, partnership monetization layers.

**Source files:** 04, 14, 15, 16, 17, 18

---

## 6. Community & Creator Program
**Summary:** Commerce-focused Q&A community (not entertainment-first), 80K DAU, 50K+ weekly questions, tiered creator incentives (affiliate, group-buy, in-app monetization), achieved +20% MAU growth.

**Source files:** 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15

---

## 7. B2B vs Consumer Strategy
**Summary:** Two-stage GTM: Consumer app proves value/PMF, then B2B partnerships (Zillow, Redfin, 직방, 다방) deliver stable recurring revenue. Consumer lifecycle = transaction-based; B2B = ongoing professional need.

**Source files:** 10, 14, 15, 17, 18

---

## 8. Go-to-Market & Execution Strategy
**Summary:** Real estate platform integration as primary GTM lever. Phased rollout: consumer PMF validation → ecosystem partner integration → white-label enterprise offerings. Speed and iteration emphasized.

**Source files:** 02, 10, 16, 18

---

## 9. 3C Flywheel Connection
**Summary:** Rebalanced existing 3C structure: Community Q&A drives inflow via SEO, Creator programs drive conversion confidence, Commerce closes sales. Achieved breakeven through this rebalancing, not new expansion.

**Source files:** 10, 11, 12, 13

---

## 10. Portfolio/Pitch Strategy & Leadership Narrative
**Summary:** Frame as X→Y→Z (problem → decision → results). Show org/team impact, not just hands-on execution. Director-level positioning. L7 interview format: Clarify context → Strategic framework → Business impact.

**Source files:** 10, 11, 17, 18

---

## 11. Team Structure & Collaboration
**Summary:** Room Planner + AI teams merged. Single UX writer orchestrated 800-person org through AI tooling. Research embedded in product cycles. Cross-functional autonomy with alignment (not command-and-control).

**Source files:** 11, 18

---

## 12. Challenges & Unresolved Questions
**Summary:** Scaling beyond Korea difficult. Lifestyle Expansion precedent = failure to ignore. AI rendering accuracy vs. user expectations tradeoff. Consumer repeat usage (one-time vs. lifecycle). Profitability on consumer side uncertain.

**Source files:** 04, 10, 11, 16, 18

---

## 13. Metrics & Success Criteria
**Summary:** D+7 retention benchmark: 40-50% (solid for utility tools). Community DAU ~80K, MAU +20% growth. Leading metrics (adoption, dwell, answer rate) → lagging metrics (conversion, LTV, retention). OMTM depends on phase.

**Source files:** 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15, 17, 18

---

## 14. Market Size & Business Opportunity
**Summary:** Virtual staging market $260M (2024) → $1.32B (2033, 17.8% CAGR). Home remodeling $545.6B. Real estate transaction = highest budget commitment moment. Global expansion potential beyond Korea.

**Source files:** 03, 04, 05, 06, 16
