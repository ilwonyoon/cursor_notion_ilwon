- **[Solutions] 그래서 장기적으로 어떤 밸류를 제공하는지를 바로 보여주는게 좋을 것이여**
    
    **1️⃣ Property Management (Before Move-in)** — *Help property platforms and owners visualize spaces before they exist.*
    
    - **Rental:** Generate lifelike visuals from floorplans, letting renters explore units virtually.
    - **Sales:** Provide AI-staged listings that boost engagement and cut staging costs.
    
    → **Value:** Faster decisions, higher conversions, lower costs.
    
    ---
    
    **2️⃣ Pre-Move Consumers (About to Move In)** — *Give buyers confidence before they commit.*
    
    - **Visualize furniture layouts and space usage** for their future home.
    - **Estimate renovation needs** and plan realistically before moving in.
    
    → **Value:** Less uncertainty, smarter purchase decisions.
    
    ---
    
    **3️⃣ Renovators (Planning Renovations)** — *Bridge the gap between imagination and execution.*
    
    - **Test materials and design combinations** virtually before committing.
    - **Use AI visuals as executable plans** for construction partners.
    
    → **Value:** Lower rework costs, faster design-to-build handoff.
    
    ---
    
    **4️⃣ Settled Consumers (Already Moved In — Refresh Users)** — *Turn daily living into continuous design discovery.*
    
    - **Suggest small refreshes** (decor, textiles, lighting) based on their existing space.
    - **Connect directly to commerce** (C.R.A.P category).
    
    → **Value:** Higher repeat engagement, long-tail commerce conversion.

- **[Business model] - 그래서 돈은 어떻게 벌겠다는 것인지?**
    
    **1️⃣ B2C: Consumer Monetization (Short-term)**
    
    **Freemium → Subscription & Usage-based model**
    
    - **Free tier**
        
        Users casually refresh or explore their space — primarily discovering and placing products.
        
        → Monetized through *ads and affiliate partnerships.*
        
    - **Paid tier (Pay-as-you-go / High Quota subscription)**
        
        Power users such as renovators generate multiple design variations to reach the “perfect fit.”
        
        → Monetized through *credit packs and monthly subscription tiers.*
        
    - **Retention driver**
        
        Personalized feedback and style-learning loops improve design quality over time, naturally increasing session frequency and conversion to paid plans.
        
    
    ---
    
    **2️⃣ B2B: Property Management Partnerships (Mid-term)**
    
    **AI visualization tools for housing & rental platforms**
    
    - **Target**
        
        House seller and rental property sites (e.g. Redfin, Zillow, Apartments.com)
        
    - **Value proposition**
        - Reduce *vacancy rates* by enabling realistic visualization of empty units.
        - Increase *engagement time per listing*, improving conversion rates for property sales or rentals.
    - **Monetization**
        - *Subscription-based SaaS model* for property owners and managers.
        - The ROI is clear — *reducing just one day of vacancy can offset the monthly fee.*
        - Built on *Ohouse’s internal model*, keeping inference costs low and scalable.
    
    ---
    
    **3️⃣ Data-as-an-Asset: Living E-commerce Graph (Long-term)**
    
    **Building the most valuable dataset in the home & living domain**
    
    - Through *property-linked user accounts*, Ohouse AI learns:
        - Rent range, location, and space type → user’s lifestyle, spending capacity, and style preferences.
    - Creates a *contextual data bridge* between **B2B** (brands, construction, property) and **B2C** (users, commerce).
    - Over time, Ohouse AI evolves into a **Living E-commerce Graph** —
        
        mapping how people actually live, what they buy, and how products coexist in real homes.
        
    - This foundation powers superior *AI recommendation, product matching, and execution planning*, unlocking future revenue via *data APIs and predictive commerce.*
